# Privacy Policy – Max-Staging Comparison Slider

**Last Updated:** March 24, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Max-Staging Comparison Slider ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you install and use the App on your Shopify store.

## Information We Collect

### Store Information
When you install the App, we access:
- Your Shopify store domain name
- Product data (via `read_products` and `write_products` API permissions)

### Subscription Information
- We store your subscription plan status (Free or Premium) as a Shopify metafield on your app installation
- Subscription billing is handled entirely by Shopify — we do not collect or store payment card details

### What We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** collect personal information from your customers
- We do **not** track customer browsing behavior or interactions
- We do **not** use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts on your storefront
- We do **not** collect email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information
- The comparison slider runs entirely in the customer's browser with no data sent to our servers

## How We Use Information

- **Store domain**: To authenticate your store and manage your subscription
- **Product data**: To enable the comparison slider functionality within your store's theme
- **Subscription status**: To determine which features (Free vs Premium) are available to your store

## Data Storage

- Session data is stored securely on our servers to maintain your authenticated session
- Subscription status is stored as a Shopify metafield within your Shopify app installation
- We do not maintain a separate database of your store or customer information

## Third-Party Services

- **Shopify**: We operate within the Shopify platform and rely on Shopify's infrastructure for authentication, billing, and data handling
- We do **not** share your data with any other third-party services

## Data Retention and Deletion

- When you uninstall the App, your session data is automatically removed
- Shopify metafields associated with the App are deleted upon uninstallation
- We comply with Shopify's mandatory GDPR webhooks for:
  - **Customer data requests**: We respond to requests for customer data
  - **Customer data erasure**: We delete any customer-related data upon request
  - **Shop data erasure**: We delete all shop-related data within 48 hours of app uninstallation

## GDPR Compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since our App does not collect personal customer data, the scope of GDPR obligations is minimal. We handle all mandatory Shopify GDPR webhooks as required.

## CCPA Compliance

We comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We do not sell personal information. Since we do not collect customer personal data, there is no personal information to disclose or delete beyond what is outlined above.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
